Latent Manifold Detection — Visual Examples

Five visualizations illustrating how autoencoder reconstruction error separates real photographs from AI-generated images.

1. The Manifold Concept

Generated image — lies ON the manifold
Real photograph — lies OFF the manifold

Real photos contain detail the decoder cannot represent. Generated images are produced BY the decoder, so they already sit on the surface.

4. Reconstruction Error Maps — Where the Signal Lives

hot = large error

Error concentrates in hair, skin texture, foliage, text, and micro-edges — detail the VAE cannot reproduce.

cold = near-zero error

Near-zero residual almost everywhere. The image already lives on the manifold.
